Mapo Tofu

Description

Classic spicy and mouth numbing dish from Sichuan

Ingredients

  • 80-120g of Ground Beef
  • 1 Block of Tofu
  • Spring Onion for garnish
  • 3 Tablespoons of Doubanjiang
  • 2 Teaspoons Sichuan Peppercorn
  • 1 Tablespoon of Fermented Black Beans
  • 2 Cloves of Garic
  • 1 Knob Ginger
  • 2 teaspoon Shaoxing Wine
  • 1 teaspoon light soy sauce
  • 250ml of Chicken Stock

Steps

  1. Toast Sichuan Peppercorns in a small skillet and ground
  2. Heat oil and fry beef until dark brown and crispy
  3. slowly heat doubanjiang in oil until oil turns red
  4. Add spring onion whites, garlic, ginger, and fermented black beans and combine
  5. add chicken stock and preboiled tofu and let simmer for 7 minutes
  6. Add seasonings like soy sauce, shaoxing, the ground peppercorns and serve
