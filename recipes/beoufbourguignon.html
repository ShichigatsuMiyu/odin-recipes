Boeuf Bourguignon

Description

The iconic french braised dish. Beef simmered for hours in the red wine of Burgundy

Ingredients

  • 1.2-1.5kg of Beef Cut suited for braising
  • 3 Large Carrots
  • 1 Large Onion
  • 4 Cloves of Garlic
  • 150g of Bacon cut into thick pieces
  • 400g of Mushroom
  • 225g of Pearl Onions
  • Parsley for garnish
  • 500ml of Chicken Broth
  • A bottle of dry red wine
  • 15ml of Fish Sauce
  • 15ml of Soy Sauce
  • Thyme, Rosmary and Bay Leaf

Steps

  1. Brown Beef evenly in a Dutch oven andremvove
  2. Lower heat and add carrot, onion and garlic until browned
  3. Add red wine, stock, fish sauce, soy sauce and aromatics alongside Beef cut into smaller pieces
  4. Bring to a simmer, transfer to oven and cook for 2 hours ntil beef becomes tender
  5. Cook bacon in a seperate skillet and transfer to plate
  6. Cook Mushroom over medium high heat until browned and add diced carrots and pearl onions
  7. Add seperate ingredients to the dutch oven, remove from heat and serve!
