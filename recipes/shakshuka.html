Shakshuka

Description

A delicous and savory dish common all over the middle east. Best served with some fluffy bread to dip!

Ingredients

  • 2 Red Bell Pepper
  • 1 Large Onion
  • 800g Or 1 large Can of Peeled Tomatoes
  • 3-5 Eggs
  • Feta Cheese
  • Olives
  • Parsley
  • (Optional) Chorizo
  • Spices: Smoked Paprika, Cumin, Garlic

Steps

  1. Dice Onion and Bell Pepper while heating up some Olive Oil over medium heat in a large Pan
  2. Add onion and red pepper to pan and cook until browned and softened
  3. Lower heat and add the garlic, smoked paprika and cumin. Combine and let it the aromas develop for a minute or two
  4. Crush tomatoes and add them to the pan. Let it simmer for 10 minutes
  5. Make little wholes in the mixture and add the eggs. Cover with a lid and let it steam until the eggs are poached
  6. Remove from heat, add toppings such as olives, feta or chorizo. Hit it with a some salt and black pepper and serve with fluffy bread!
